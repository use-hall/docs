When you sign up for Hall, your community site will automatically be created. 

Your community site is accessible on its own unique `.hall.community` subdomain, or on a [custom domain](/custom-domains) if configured. To see your community site, simply click the **Your community site** button in the left navigation of the [admin dashboard](https://dashboard.usehall.com).
