## Set up channels

To get started with Hall, you'll first need to enable the channels you want to use to provide community support for your customers.

When you first sign up, the [Support forum](/channels/support-forum) will be enabled.

## Explore and customize your community site

When enabled, these channels will be accessible on your [community site](/community-site).
