---
title: Bot auto-reply
description: Configure the auto-reply for new messages in Discord
---
When new messages are posted in the channels you are [syncing](./syncing), you can enable an automated reply.

This automated reply includes a link to relevant answers that we have found in your [community site](/community-site) using our [answer engine](/answer-engine).

This helps people to find answers to their questions while they wait for someone to reply.

<Frame>
![Auto-reply in Discord](/images/discord-auto-reply.png)
</Frame>

This automated reply will come from the "Hall" bot/app user in your Discord server.
